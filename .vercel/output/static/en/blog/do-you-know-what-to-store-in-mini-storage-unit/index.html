Do you know what to store in a mini storage unit?

If you still don't know what things you can store, here we share smart object ideas: save space, optimize order and protect the essential without complications.

2 min

If you still don't know what things you can store, here we share smart object ideas: save space, optimize order and protect the essential without complications. Keep reading and transform your chaos into efficiency.

Furniture

They are the most common objects in storage: chairs, tables, closets, pictures, lamps, among others. Useful during a remodeling or if you use the space at home during a remodeling or a temporary move. Clean them before storing and cover them with light fabrics or plastics helps avoid dust and scratches.

Electronic and office equipment

Devices that are not used frequently such as televisions, printers, microwaves or computers. It's best to store them in climate-controlled storage units to avoid damage from extreme heat, free up space in your business or home and keep them safe.

Books, documents and files

They are ideal for storing in our storage units: they help free up space, maintain order and preserve what you value most. Just make sure to pack them properly, use cardboard or plastic boxes and you'll have efficient and durable storage, without complications.

Tools and DIY materials

From wrenches and drills to spare parts, mini storage units are perfect for keeping them organized and out of the way.

Sports equipment and suitcases

Bicycles, mats, camping items or suitcases take up space at home, but fit well in storage units, freeing up valuable space.

Memories or items with sentimental value

Photos, albums, old toys, collections or antique furniture. These items have sentimental value, so it's essential to store them in appropriate boxes, protected from heat, humidity or possible scratches.
