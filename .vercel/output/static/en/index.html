Professional storage tips & best practices from experts

Identify cluttered areas in your home filled with items you rarely use. A self storage unit can free up valuable living space while keeping your belongings safe and accessible.

The volume and type of items determine your ideal storage unit size. Choose small units for documents, seasonal items, and sports equipment, or larger units for furniture, appliances, and household goods.

Ensure all boxes, tape, and packing materials are high-quality and in excellent condition. Protect fragile items with bubble wrap, packing paper, or moving blankets.

  • • Fill boxes completely to prevent crushing.
  • • Use crumpled paper or bubble wrap to fill empty spaces.
  • • Sprinkle baking soda to eliminate odors.
  • • Plan your move ahead of time to minimize damage - you can count on our moving service for safe transport.
  • • We provide unloading assistance and free use of dollies and hand trucks.
  • • Your vehicle can drive right up to your storage unit, warehouse, or locker door.
  • • Fill boxes completely to prevent them from collapsing.
  • • Make a content list and label each box.
  • • Place heavy boxes on the bottom and light ones on top.
